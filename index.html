Hello! I'm Shams Tabrez, a passionate photographer based in Bhagalplur, Bihar, India. With a love for storytelling and a keen eye for detail, I specialize in capturing moments that are as authentic as they are beautiful. Photography has been my lifelong passion. I believe in the power of light and shadow to create depth and mood in my photos, and I'm constantly inspired by the world around me. When I'm not behind the lens, you can often find me exploring the great outdoors, seeking out new adventures and hidden gems to photograph and I believe that my experiences outside of photography enrich my work and allow me to see the world in new and exciting ways.
